Baked Scalloped Potatoes

Home

Description

Baked scalloped potatoes with mushroom soup go well with any entrée, whether fancy or plain. Use whatever type of condensed cream soup you like — I normally switch it up based on what I'm serving it with.

Prep Time: 25 mins

Cook Time: 1 hr

Total Time: 1hr 25mins

PServing: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 ¼ cups milk, or more as needed
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 6 large potatoes, peeled and sliced
  • 1 medium onion, diced

Directions

Step 1 Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a 2-quart casserole dish

Step 2 Whisk condensed soup, milk, and pepper together in a bowl.

Step 3 Layer potatoes and onion in the prepared casserole dish. Pour soup mixture over top; it should almost cover potatoes and onion. If it doesn't, add more milk.

Step 4 Cover and bake in the preheated 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) oven until potatoes are tender, about 1 hour, stirring halfway through.
