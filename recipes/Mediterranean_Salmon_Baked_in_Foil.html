Mediterranean Salmon Baked in Foil

Home

Description

This Mediterranean salmon, baked in foil packets with olives, artichokes, tomatoes, and Mediterranean seasonings, stays moist and tender, and cleanup is minimal.

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 30 mins

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 (12x16-inch) rectangles aluminum foil
  • 4 (6 to 8 ounce) salmon fillets
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil, or as needed, divided
  • 1 teaspoon Greek seasoning
  • 1 cup diced cherry or grape tomatoes
  • 4 marinated artichoke hearts, quartered
  • 3 tablespoons minced red onion
  • 3 tablespoons halved, pitted Kalamata olives
  • 1/2 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh parsley, plus parsley sprigs for garnish (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges (optional)

Directions

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Brush the center of each foil rectangle with a small amount of olive oil.

Step 2: Place a salmon fillet in the center of each foil sheet. Brush fillets lightly with olive oil; sprinkle with Greek seasoning.

Step 3: In a small bowl, combine tomatoes, artichokes, onion, olives, lemon zest and juice, minced parsley, balsamic vinegar, garlic, red pepper flakes, and 1 tablespoon olive oil. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

Step 4: Spoon tomato mixture evenly over fillets. Pull the long ends of foil rectangles over fillets and fold twice to crimp, making sure the foil does not touch the contents. Pinch the ends closed and place packets on a rimmed baking sheet.

Step 5: Bake in the center of the preheated oven for 12 to 15 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center of the fillets should read 145°F (63°C).

Step 6: To serve, place foil packs on serving plates. Open packets, drizzle with any remaining olive oil, garnish with parsley sprigs, and serve with lemon wedges.
